Thinking in Russian

Make a list of the things you do ALL the time. For me its:

  1. I make and drink coffee.
  2. I sit down and meditate.
  3. I eat bread.
  4. I take a piss.

Now learn to say these phrases in Russian; Start with pronouns,
then objects then verbs.


  1. Я делаю и пыю кофе
  2. Я Сажусь и медитирую
  3. Я ем Хлеб
  4. Я Писаю
